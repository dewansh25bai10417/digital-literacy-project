## **Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist**

1. Always verify links before clicking—check the URL carefully.
2. Never share OTPs, passwords, or PINs with anyone.
3. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all accounts.
4. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or payments.
5. Install apps only from official app stores.
6. For UPI safety: Double-check the receiver’s name before sending money.
7. For UPI safety: Never approve “collect requests” from unknown sources.
8. Keep your phone and apps updated to avoid security vulnerabilities.
9. Log out from shared or public devices after use.
10. Report suspicious messages or fraud immediately.
